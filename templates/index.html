Why giving books?

Giving comes in a lot of different forms and it is one of the most happy way to increase life happiness. On the other hand, people mostly read a book once and then leaved it on the shelves. So there's no reason to just let such an amazing thing covered with grime, we can alternately give it to others!

Book Giving Project

Book Giving Project is a platform provide you an ability to connect to people in your location. You can either giving your books out or recieving books from others. All you wanna do is go to the Gallery section to see what others want to give out or go to the Giving section to post your book to the local user.

How to use?

- Go to Gallery to see what book others have posted.

- Go to Giving to giving your book out

- Go to Blogs to share your experiences and connect with each others
